Pizza margherita

A pizza margherita is the ultimate Italian comfort food, simple yet delicious. To make it all that is needed is the following.

Ingredients

  • Typo 00 flower
  • Yeast, active or dry
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make dough balls
  2. Create the pizza
  3. Bake the pizza at 320 degrees C for a couple of minutes
  4. Enjoy!
